Try the Widget

Click the floating button in the bottom-right corner to open the chat.

Integration — one script tag

Add the following snippet before the closing </body> tag on any page. The floating button appears automatically.

<!-- Vaquill floating chatbot --> <script src="https://your-widget-host/embed/script-floating-chatbot.js" data-api-url="https://your-widget-host" data-primary-color="#1a56db" ></script>

No other changes are required. The widget is self-contained and does not modify any existing page elements.

What Vaquill can answer

Ask anything related to Indian law — case law, statutes, constitutional provisions, and more:

  • What is Section 302 of the Indian Penal Code?
  • Explain the scope of Article 21 after Maneka Gandhi v. Union of India.
  • What are the grounds for divorce under the Hindu Marriage Act, 1955?
  • Summarise the doctrine of promissory estoppel in Indian contract law.

Configuration options

Customise the widget via data-* attributes:

data-api-url → Backend URL (required in production) data-primary-color → Brand hex colour (default: #1a56db) data-button-size → Button diameter (default: 60px) data-chat-width → Panel width (default: 400px) data-chat-height → Panel height (default: 600px)
